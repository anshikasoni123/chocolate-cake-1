Ingrediant Amount
Baking spray 1 bottle
Butter 1 stick
bittersweet chocolate 2 ounces
Semi sweet chocolate 2 ounces
powdered sugar 1 and 1/4 cups
eggs 2
egg yolks 3
vannila essence 1 teaspoon
all purpose flour 1/2 of a cup
vannila ice cream 1 scoop

Directions

  • Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F. Spray four custard cups with baking spray and place on a baking sheet.
  • Microwave the butter, bittersweet chocolate and semisweet chocolate in a large bowl on high until the butter is melted, about 1 minute. Whisk until the chocolate is also melted. Stir in the sugar until well blended. Whisk in the eggs and egg yolks, then add the vanilla flavouring. Stir in the flour. Divide the mixture among the cups.
  • Bake until the sides are firm and the centers are soft, about 13 minutes. Let cool down for 1 minute.serve with vanilla ice cream.
